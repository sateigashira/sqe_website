About

The Society of Quantum Engineers at SJSU was started as a branch of the SQE org at the Colorado School of Mines. We aim to act as advocators for Quantum Information Science education and hope to suport a community of quantum enthusiasts from all disciplines.

We welcome you, whether you are interested in pursuing Quantum Information Science as a career or as a hobby! In addition to supporting a healthy community of quantum enthusiasts, we also hope to support the Masters in Quantum Technology Program (MSQT) here at SJSU.

Quantum Seminar Series

The seminar series was started to help build a community of quantum enthusiasts and give students an opportunity to learn about advancements in this field.

Speakers include industry professionals from many companies in silicon valley as well as professors from different institutions

Are you a quantum professional or professor doing exciting research? Feel free to contact us if you're interested in giving a talk! We always try to work around speakers' schedules.
